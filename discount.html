KU Textbook Rental & Printing Discount Program

Exclusive discounts for students who have paid 60% or more of their tuition fees

Verify Your Eligibility

Enter your student information to check if you qualify for discounts on textbook rentals and printing services.

Note:

Your tuition payment status will be verified with the university's finance department. Eligibility discounts are applied at the point of purchase.

Discount Benefits

Students who have paid 60% or more of their tuition fees are eligible for the following discounts:

Textbook Rental 25% OFF

Rent required textbooks for the semester at a discounted rate.

Example: UGX 80,000 textbook → UGX 60,000 after discount

Printing Services 40% OFF

Get discounts on all printing services including thesis printing, course materials, and assignments.

Example: UGX 500/page → UGX 300/page after discount

Stationery Supplies 15% OFF

Discount on notebooks, pens, and other academic supplies from the campus store.

Example: UGX 15,000 notebook set → UGX 12,750 after discount

Special Package Deals

Combined textbook rental and printing packages at additional discounted rates.

Example: Textbook + 200 pages printing → 35% OFF total package

How to Claim Your Discounts

  1. Verify your eligibility using the form
  2. Present your student ID at the campus store or printing service
  3. The discount will be applied to your purchase automatically
  4. For online orders, use discount code: KU60PAID
