Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money (MTN and Airtel), credit/debit cards, cash on pickup, and student account billing for eligible items.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes, we offer a 10% student discount on most items with valid student ID. Additional discounts are available during special promotion periods.

Where do I pick up my order?

All orders can be picked up at the KU Marketplace store located in the Student Center building. You'll receive a notification when your order is ready.

Can I return or exchange items?

Yes, we have a 7-day return policy for most items with original receipt. Textbooks have different return conditions based on rental agreements.

How does textbook rental work?

Textbook rental allows you to borrow textbooks for the semester at a fraction of the purchase price. Simply return the book at the end of the semester in good condition.
